A NFT Collection To Celebrate Our Heroes

Mint

What is this about

During the pandemic, many of us lost our loved ones, our friends, our families to the dreadful virus that took away many lives.

But our medical practitioners stood strong, they made sacrifices for their patients and didn’t opt out when it was tough. And so this collection of 45,000 NFTs was made to celebrate them and the courage they showed

Keep in mind that this project is not profit based and the earnings from the sales will go to charity

Roadmap

Marketing & Whitelist Sale

Let’s build the hype, spread the word, Join the community platforms. Active members on the Discord will be rewarded with a free NFT before the pre-sale. 10 NFts to be won, so be prepared

Pre-Sale

Once the whitelist sale stage ends. The pre-sale will begin and will last 3 days

The Big Reveal

At the end of the pre-sale stage, the revel will take place, revealing all unminted and previously minted NFTs

Giveaway Stage

At the end if the public sale. There would be a leaderboard of the accounts with the most NFTs. The top 20 will be sent different gifts that would be revealed in the community platforms.

Donation

The community will vote which charity organisations get the money from the primary sales(Whitelist, Pre-sale and Public sale).
